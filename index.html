Top 3 attractions of Kyiv

Motherland

Motherland is a monumental sculpture in Kyiv on the right bank of the Dnieper. It is the tallest statue on the territory of Ukraine.

Kiev-Pechersk Lavra

The Kiev-Pechersk Lavra is one of the first monasteries of Kievan Rus to be founded. One of the most important Orthodox shrines, the third Lot of the Virgin Mary.

Church of St. Nicholas

Church of St. Nicholas, House of Organ and Chamber Music is a Roman Catholic church in Kyiv, used since 1980 as the House of Organ and Chamber Music.
